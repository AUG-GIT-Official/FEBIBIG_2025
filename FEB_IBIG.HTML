ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

As we celebrate Valentine's Day, the FEB-IBIG: Love Stories Unveiled is an online platform where students of the University of the Cordilleras-Integrated School can anonymously share their mala-MMK stories with a maximum of 8 sentences. Topics may cover different kinds of love (e.g. Family Love, Platonic Love, Romantic Love). This website, in collaboration with the Artificial Universe Guild, STEM Society, Civitas, and ASSETS, aims to foster a safe and respectful environment by enforcing rules and regulations to prevent cyberbullying and other issues.

RULES AND REGULATION

Rules and regulations are enforced to ensure peace, foster mutual respect, and maintain a safe, inclusive, and harmonious environment for everyone. These guidelines are designed to promote positive interactions, prevent conflicts, and protect the well-being of all individuals, ensuring that everyone feels valued, respected, and secure.

1. No Name-Dropping of Classmates or Peers

EXAMPLE: ANNA FROM SECTION 11-X GEORGE HAS CRUSH ON YOU

Respect someone's privacy, keep their secrets, and maintain anonymity in this platform to avoid cyberbullying. If you wish to identify a person, cover them with a nickname.

2. No Sexual or Inappropriate Comments

EXAMPLE: ANNA FROM SECTION - X YOUR SO SEXY

Respect someone's privacy, dignity, and boundaries. Avoid making comments about a person's physical appearance and personal life. Furthermore, avoid any comments that could be interpreted as inappropriate or offensive. Maintain a professional and respectful environment for everyone.

3. No Hate Speech or Harassment

EXAMPLE: ANNA FROM SECTION - X YOUR SO UGLY

Hate speech, bullying, or harrassment, in any form or kind, will not be tolerated. This includes, but not limited to, comments about someone's appearance, race, gender, religion, culture, or personal characteristics. Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Any form of hateful speech or harmful behavior that is detected will result in immediate consequences.
